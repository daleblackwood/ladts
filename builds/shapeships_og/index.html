ShapeShips v0.9

LAD.js demo by Dale J Williams

Fly your paper-plane-typed ship for as long as possible, fighting off wave after wave of gradually improving enemies. Become the master of some kind of space paper environment.

Controls

Move ShipW, A, S, D
ShootArrow Keys, Space or Mouse

This game runs from my engine LAD.js, is a Javascript/Canvas game, made in about twenty hours. Interesting tech includes:

  • A path-drawing framework, resembling that of graphics software.
  • Seperated game logic and rendering loops - allowing for maximum framerate whilst maintaining play speed.
  • A full engine backend - to be extended.

I've put this game, and engine LAD.js, on Google Code under a BSD license - so have at it, if you will, just remember to keep the attribution and note your changes.
